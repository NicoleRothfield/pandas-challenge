Heroes of Pymoli Analysis
-------------------------

1) Male users represent the majority of the players.

2) The age range from 15-24 years represents the majority of the players and total purchase value, but Average Total Purchase per Person is the highest in the age 35-39 group. 

3) To grow the Heroes of Pymoli game, I would suggest increasing target marketing to the male age groups from 15 - 24 year olds to grow their current target.
